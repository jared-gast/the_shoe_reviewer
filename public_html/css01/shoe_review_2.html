Nike © Air Max 97 Plus "Layer Cake"

Score:3.5/5

This is a bulky shoe. The Air Max 97 is in style, but this variation of the 97 forgoes the sleek base design. Not the most comfortable shoe. If you can find the shoe for a good price it is a decent buy, but we would not recommend using it as an everday shoe.
