Nike Free RN Flyknit

Review

If you're looking for a casual shoe to wear around the house, this shoe is for you! This shoe is very comfy and flexible. With just fabric above the sole this shoe is very breathable, perfect to wear around the house on a lazy Saturday or for a walk thorugh a park. The downside, with the only fabric style the shoe lacks any support. Would not recommend to go lift weights or to go on a run with the Free RN Flyknit. A must buy for a casual shoe however, its lack of versatility hurts the final score. Overall, a rating of 3.5/5 for the Free RN Flyknit.
