The Shoe Reviewer

Nike Revolution 5

Review

Can't go wrong with the Nike Revolution 5. Whether you are looking for a casual shoe to wear around the house, a comfortable sturdy shoe to workout in, or a shoe to wear out on the town to catch people's eyes, the Nike Revoultion 5 will accomplish whatever you need. At first glance the all white shoe looks fresh and clean. The blue nike swish and lime green add fresh color. When first worn, the shoe insole feels comfortable and padded at the bottom. A solid 4.8/5 stars for the Nike Revolution 5!
